Sanskrit-Hindi Accessor cum Machine Translator follows the traditional method of Khandaanvaya(खण्डान्वय) and the essential factors for verbal cognition (शाब्दबोध) viz. आकांक्षा (Expectancy), योग्यता (Mutual Congruity), सन्निधि (Proximity) and तात्पर्य (Purport) to analyse the given Sanskrit text and then produces its translation into Hindi. Machine shows the analysis at each of the following steps.
  • Padapaatha
  • Padaparicaya
  • Aakaankshaa
  • Yogyataa
  • Sannidhi
  • Taatparya
